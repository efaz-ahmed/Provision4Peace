About Provision4Peace

It all begins with an idea.

Provision4Peace is a specialised funeral membership fund company focused on providing peace of mind to members and their loved ones. The organisation combines funeral financial security with ethical investment principles.

Our origin

A scheme born of need.

Welcome to Provision4Peace, also known as P4P.

The idea behind Provision4Peace was born during the COVID-19 pandemic. The concept developed from an exclusive scheme originally offered by New Vision Housing Limited in 2018 to its members. Provision4Peace now offers these opportunities nationwide across the UK.

  1. 2018

    An exclusive NVH scheme

    The original concept launches as a members-only offering inside New Vision Housing Limited, providing a faith-conscious route to funeral cost preparation.

  2. 2020 · pandemic

    A wider need recognised

    As COVID-19 brought the question of end-of-life provision into sharp focus, it became clear the scheme’s principles could ease the burden for families well beyond NVH’s own membership.

  3. Today

    Nationwide across the UK

    Provision4Peace now offers ethical funeral membership plans to households across the United Kingdom, retaining the original commitment to Shari’ah-conscious principles.

Our mission

Financial security, with dignity.

Our mission is to deliver financial security for funeral expenses through options aligned with ethical standards and responsible practices. Provision4Peace supports individuals and communities in gaining peace of mind during difficult times while making a positive impact on society.

Our vision

Prosperity and peace, together.

Our vision is to become a trusted leader in funeral investment funds, known for integrity, transparency and client satisfaction. We aim to create a financial ecosystem where prosperity and peace coexist.

Financial security and peace of mind

Easing the burden, when it counts most.

Provision4Peace aims to reduce the emotional and financial pressure placed on families at the time of death. Through funeral financial planning, members can help their loved ones manage end-of-life expenses in a dignified and responsible way.

Ethical commitment

A philosophy, not a feature.

Ethical membership is not just a strategy for Provision4Peace; it is part of the organisation’s core philosophy. Financial opportunities are evaluated against strict ethical criteria to ensure alignment with responsible and Shari’ah-compliant principles.
