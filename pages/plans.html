Funeral Membership Fund Plans

Choose a funeral membership fund plan designed to help your loved ones cover funeral expenses through an ethical and Shari’ah-compliant structure.

How it works

A 70/30 structure.

All Provision4Peace plans are based on a 70/30 ratio. The member contributes 70% of the chosen fund value into the membership fund. Provision4Peace aims to contribute the remaining 30% at the time of death.

The 70/30 structure is illustrative because fixed returns would not align with Shari’ah principles. The member is guaranteed to receive their initial 70% contribution. Funds are invested into 100% halal ventures. The primary goal is funeral cost support, not profit generation.

Member 70%
Company 30%
Guaranteed initial contribution Illustrative aim
Two-year waiting period

All plans have a two-year waiting period from registration. If the member dies within two years, it is at the company’s discretion whether any return is given.
